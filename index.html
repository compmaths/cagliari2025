Partner Institutes

Hacettepe University, Department of Mathematics

Oriented to Bachelor, Master and Doctorate students

Overview

In this program, an intensive 5-day, 30-hour course which will be held at the University of Cagliari on " Theoretical and numerical analysis of differential equations and their applications" at the undergraduate and graduate levels will be offered, prepared by professors from the University of Cagliari and partner universities. This project aims to deepen the study of differential equations, both from a theoretical and numerical perspective, with particular attention to solving problems in scientific and engineering contexts as focusing on the analysis of differential equations applied to real-world models.

Participants who complete the course will receive 3 ECTS credits.
